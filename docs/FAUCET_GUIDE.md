# LucrativeClix Coin (LCC) Faucet Guide

Welcome to the official **LucrativeClix Coin (LCC) faucet guide**.  
This document explains how to safely claim LCC tokens on the Sepolia testnet.

---

## 1. Prerequisites

Before claiming LCC, make sure you have:

1. **A Web3 wallet** (e.g., MetaMask)  
2. **Sepolia Ethereum Testnet selected** in your wallet  
3. Optional: **Some Sepolia ETH** to cover gas fees (if needed)  
   - You can get Sepolia ETH from public faucets like [Alchemy Faucet](https://faucets.alchemy.com/sepolia)

---

## 2. How to Claim LCC

1. Open the **LucrativeClix faucet website**:  
   [https://lucrativeclix.vercel.app](https://lucrativeclix.vercel.app)

2. Connect your **wallet** using MetaMask or WalletConnect

3. Click the **“Claim Free LCC”** button

4. Wait for the transaction to complete

5. Check your wallet balance — your LCC tokens should appear instantly

---

## 3. Claim Limits

- Users can claim **100 LCC every 24 hours**  
- Faucet enforces a cooldown to prevent abuse  
- If your claim fails, wait until the cooldown period expires  

---

## 4. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|---------|
| Faucet says “Transaction failed” | Ensure you are on Sepolia network and have gas ETH |
| LCC not appearing in wallet | Add token manually using contract address: `0x873612265e161c3857d9223B1BA06Eb98D9dE65a` |
| MetaMask won’t connect | Clear browser cache or try WalletConnect |
